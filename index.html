About

My Journey from Health to Tech

After graduating with a bachelors in Public Health at University of the Philippines Manila, I started my journey in health research where I developed an interest in understanding how data can be used in decision making and improving outcomes. Over time, I grew more passionate about data and analytics. To explore this further, I joined a data analytics bootcamp from Eskwelabs where I learned SQL, data storytelling, and visualization. I found the process enjoyable and rewarding! It always made me excited to see how data can be used to uncover insights and drive change.

Because of this experience, I decided to pursue an Associates degree in Software Engineering at Oakland Community College to further develop my skills and explore how I can use technology to make data more accessible and useful. Now, as a Software Engineering student, I’m building my skills in programming and exploring how data, health, and technology can work together. I enjoy analyzing information, solving problems, and creating systems that make data more useful and accessible.
